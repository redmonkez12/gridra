# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in communication.
- Assume good intent, but address technical disagreement directly.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code of conduct.
